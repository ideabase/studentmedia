Our Mission

Award-winning, nationally recognized and locally focused. Our independent, student-run media partners create content the campus and Kent community seek. Students learn practical skills through the professional learning opportunities available through Kent State Student Media. Advertising with Kent State Student Media is an excellent way to make sure you reach your audience.
