Color Pricing

Volume Discounts

  • Clients may sign volume discounts per semester.
  • The client must run a minimum of the contracted amount to receive the discountfor all Kent State ads.

  • $750 = 5% off
  • $1,250 = 10% off
  • $1,500 = 15% off
  • $2,500+ = 20% off

Display Ad Deadlines

  • Ad space reservation is due two business days prior to the run date.
  • Ad copy is due one business dayprior to the run date.
  • Earlier deadlines may apply for special projects or sections.

Inserts & Post-Its®

  • Single-Page Insert: $450
  • Multi-Page Insert: $500
  • 3'x3' or 4'x5' Post-It®: $600
  • Post-Its® must be printed in a fan-fold and boxed.
  • The unwind direction must be #1 or #3.
  • Insert/Post-It® sample must be submitted to Kent State Student Media for approval. Inserts/Post-Its® must be printed by the client and shipped directly to the printer at least one week prior to insertion.
  • Inserts and Post-Its® are within every copy of The Kent Stater. Advertisers should print and ship 6,750 total.
